PÉ NO CHÃO! Investing

Target

Every year, a large contingent of people are:

  • Becoming better financially educated
  • Looking for new ways to make their investments

These investors are tired of using old financial services that:

  • Charge them high fees
  • Do not present them with the best investment options available
  • Are committed to the profit of banks or brokers and not to generate the best results for investors
  • Do not respect the investor's needs, according to their objectives
  • Do not be precisely informed about the risks

Pain

When getting rid of banks, some investors will soon find that:

  • Managing an investment portfolio can involve dealing with dozens of assets at the same time.
  • This work consumes a lot of time, which could be used in many more useful ways.
  • The lack of a clear investment strategy can lead to act based on their momentaneal emotional states (many times guided by euphoria or fear and anxiety) and therefore poor financial decisions.
  • Investment spreadsheets demand a lot of attention to be filled out, offer little automation and cannot handle the complexity of implementing certain investment strategies.

Solution

"PÉ NO CHÃO INVESTING" will be developed as a Financial Advisor to provide the following solutions:

  • Interactive tool to know the investor's profile: age, income, financial objectives, initial capital to be invested, monthly income available for new investments, etc.
  • Predict the most appropriate strategy for the investor, according to their profile. The Strategies are always conservative (Pé No Chão!)
  • Indicate the initial asset allocation.
  • At each scheduled period, rebalance the portfolio, indicating the assets to be bought and sold and their quantities
  • When necessary investor will be advised (by email, sms, whatsapp, etc.) in order of do some action
  • Keep a full history of all operations and asset positions
  • And more...
